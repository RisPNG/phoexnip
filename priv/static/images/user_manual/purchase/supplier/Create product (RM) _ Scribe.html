    Create product (RM)

    • Cees Kettenis |
    • 16 steps |
    • 31 seconds
    1
    Click "Product Management".
    2
    Click "New".
    3
    Fill in the "Number" field.
    The number must be unique!
    4
    FIll in the "Name" Field.
    5
    Fill in the "Unit" field.
    6
    Select at least one colour.
    7
    Select which season this product is for.
    8
    Click "Supplier Information".
    9
    Click "Add" to add supplier information.
    10
    Select supplier by Code or Name.
    11
    Fill in the "MOQ" for this supplier.
    12
    Fill in "Lead Time (days)" for this supplier.
    13
    Select an "Effective Date"
    14
    Fill in "MCQ" for this supplier
    15
    Fill in "Surcharge" for this supplier.
    Info: Repeat step 9 till 15 until all suppliers are present.
    16
    Click "Save" once all the information is filled in.
